Sea World

Visit our SeaWorld and experience a great adventure into the deep ocean where you will meet amazing sea animals like the Stingray and the Great white shark. Read more about Dragons Park Sea World.

Dragons Park Sea World

We have reconstructed Dragons Park Sea World so that our Sea animals can live in a pure and natural looking habitat. Dragons Park Sea World also features new technology that makes it possible for guests to walk or swim safely amongst Sea animals such as the Great White Shark by making guest invincible to some category of Sea animals.

Our new technology also makes it possible for guest cloths to remain 100% dry even when under water.
