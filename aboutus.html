About Us

Dragons Park

Dragons Park is the only UK theme park to be awarded the Gold Award for 10 consecutive years, by the Trip Advocators Organization, based on millions of valuable reviews and opinions of the organizations members around the world.

The Park opened in June 2000, attracting over 30,000 guest on the opening day alone and today the Park attracts over 80,000 guests every day.

We work very hard to continually introduce new rides and attractions to the park and we have some great and exciting plans scheduled for 2018.

We pride ourselves on being a family-friendly Park that is affordable and caters for all ages including the disabled.

Dragons Park has over 50 rides and attractions specially designed and build exclusively for the Park. The Park has 500 acres of parkland with a river running through it and a waterfall. It is the only park in the world where you can still find real Dragons that existed more than 200,000,000 years ago, such as the Blue Dragon and Vishap.
