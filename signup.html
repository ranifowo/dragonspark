Dragons Park News

This month, Dragons Park will unveil the original Dragons Den exactly as it existed more than 200,000,000 years ago, giving guests the opportunity to look around and see for the first time exactly what happened inside the original Dragons Den.

The Queen and the King of Dragons live inside the Den. As guests approach the Den, the first thing they will notice is the presence of two Elephants, these elephants guard the Queen and the King of Dragons, they are the first line of defence and they protect the treasures inside the Den.

If the elephants grant guests permission to enter the Den, they will be met by 10 Blue Dragons, these Blues Dragons are the second line of defence, they are capable of reading peoples mind and can tell if any guest visiting the Den has good or bad intensions.

After passing the Blue Dragons you then need to pass through a cave which is about ½ a kilometre long, leading to the palace of the Queen and King of all Dragons. The floor and wall of this cave are decorated with solid gold tiles and the cave is lit by pure and very bright diamonds.

Once you’re inside the Den what you will see and experience will provide you with full knowledge of the history of Dragons and you will be able to answer many questions regarding these mythical creatures called Dragons.
