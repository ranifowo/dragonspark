Transportation

Bus Service

Catch the Arriva 100 bus from Hartfeild, Watford or St. Albans and exit at Lister Hospital.

Train station and Taxi service

Our nearest train station is Stevenage (approx. 2 miles away). Bentleys Taxi's are located outside Stevenage train station, offering a professional service. They can provide 1 to 8 seater wheelchair accessible vehicles. Contact them on: 01438 777777 W: www.bentlyscars.co.uk
