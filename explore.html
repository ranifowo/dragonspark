Explore

By staying at Dragons Park Hotel you’ll be close to all our rides and attractions and will benefit from 50% discount on second day tickets. Our rooms are luxurious and accessible to the disabled.

Dragons Park Restaurant offer something to suit even the wildest appetite, from pizza and pasta to oriental and Mexican dishes.

You can relax at our heated outdoor swimming pool or you can enjoy a home-made fruit sundae or a cool drink while relaxing by the pool or on our beautiful lawn for sunbathing.

Dragons Carousel Ride provides guests with a great experience. Everyone from children to adults, can ride on one of our many Dragon Horses.

Dragons Twisting Track travels at speeds of up to 200mph to escape fire from some wild dragons in the park.

The best way to explore Dragons Park is to fly on one of our friendly dragons. Our dragons will take you to parts of the park that can’t be reached on foot such as the Alligator Lake Volcano.

Young children and the elderly can travel quickly around the park by taking the Dragons Park Train.

Relax and unwind with your family while exploring and discovering Dragons Park from the comfort of a horse-drawn carriage.

One of the Kids favourite ride and a great family ride in Dragons Park is the Teacup Spin. Each cup can take up to 5 people and there are 8 cups in total.
