Facilities

Disabled Guests

Disabled guests are welcome! We will do our best to ensure disabled guests are able to participate in almost all activities and have a pleasurable and save visit. Please ask for an Accessibility Guide leaflet once you arrive.

We offer the following facilities for the disabled:

  • Free admission for guest dependent on wheelchair or mobility scooter
  • All our rides are designed to be accessible to disabled guests.
  • Careers for disabled guest get 50% off admission fee
  • All paths have a flat and tarmac surface
  • Special car park area for the disabled
  • Ample toilet facilities for guests with disabilities
  • Restaurants, shops, toilets are wheelchair accessible

Scooters and wheelchairs are available to hire from our first aid centre free of charge. Availability is limited during busy periods so please book online before your visit or by calling 08008002.

First Aid Centre

We have a dedicated First Aid Centre with a trained First Aid Doctor. It is located close to the main entrance of the park.

We also administer our Queue Assist Scheme and coordinate our Lost Children Procedure at our At First Aid Centre.

Car Park

Our Car Park is just 50 metres away from the main entrance. This parking area can easily be found by following the clear signs from the main drive. Our car park can accommodate 5000 cars, each individual parking bay is clearly marked and Parking is free for all guests.

Restaurant

Dragons Park Restaurant offer something to suit even the wildest appetite, from pizza and pasta to oriental and Mexican dishes.

Dragons Park Restaurant has a Bar full of drinks for all ages.

Hotel

Stay over at the Dragons Park Hotel and get exclusive early ride time to all rides.

We offer good rates for trip planners, corporate guests and travellers. Please call our dedicated call centre on 08008001
